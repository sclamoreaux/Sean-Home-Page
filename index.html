BIO

I was born in Oregon and was raised primarily in Oro Valley, Arizona. I was an artistic kid, frequently drawing, painting, or doing arts and crafts projects. I graduated from Canyon Del Oro High School, where I was actively involved in theater and music programs. I later moved to New York City to finish college and experience big city life.

I happily returned to Arizona in 2014 and my love for desert life was rekindled. In hopes of applying my creative mind to a good career, I began studying web development independently in fall of 2015. I specialize in front-end technologies and believe that good web design should be both functional for the user and expressive of the client.
